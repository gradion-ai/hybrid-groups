Register Hybrid Groups Slack App

This setup guide will walk you through the process of registering the Hybrid Groups Slack app in your Slack workspace.

You need a Slack account with admin permissions to continue.

Go to api.slack.com/apps, login to your account and navigate to the section Your App Configuration Tokens.

Click on Generate Token and copy the generated Access Token.
